Frequently Asked Questions

Return to Instruction Manual

How do I search for cards?

Enter text into the search bar and press Search. The search matches card names, descriptions, sets, and image filenames.

How do I view a card?

Click the card image on the card search page to view details on the individual card page.

How do I change my account?

Go to User Settings to update your username or password, log out, or delete your account.
